Candy Jar - Tea Light Lantern

Inspiration: Walking between the shelves and stop in front of a candy jar of smiley faces, Smiles were squeezed into sadness, madness, evilness... A person wearing a smiling mask, walking inside the crowd, the transformation of the face, the mask the one is wearing. What's being exposure, what's being hidden? The distortion, and what's real from the light coming from the inner self.

Lotus flower Lake - Plate

Inspiration: Sunlight going down hitting the ground and reflecting the heat upward in a hot summer day. A lotus flower blooming on a peaceful lake. Casually a soft cloud would move and cover the sun with all the water molecules it's carrying. Suddenly breeze coming by gentally over the surface of the lake. Bringing up weak ripples around the lotus flower. The pink sways slightly, quietly.

Threads - WindChime
